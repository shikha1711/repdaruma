Our Story

History of Daruma Artisan Studio

Daruma Artisan Studio was born in the heart of Kyoto in 1998, founded by a group of passionate artisans who aimed to preserve and evolve the rich heritage of handcrafted Daruma dolls. What began as a small family-run workshop has blossomed into a studio recognized for its intricate craftsmanship and deep respect for cultural values.

Over the years, the studio has become a place where tradition meets innovation — embracing contemporary techniques while remaining rooted in the timeless philosophy of perseverance and hope. Our journey is one of dedication, where every artisan carries forward generations of skill, spirit, and purpose.

Meet the Original Artists Who Started It

Our studio may be small, but each brushstroke carries generations of belief — in falling seven times and standing up eight. Behind every Daruma is a story of discipline, patience, and pride in Japanese artistry.
